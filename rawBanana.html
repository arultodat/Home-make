Raw Banana Powder

Our Raw Banana Powder is made from naturally grown Kerala Nentharam Bananas, carefully processed to retain their rich

nutritional value. It is a perfect addition to a healthy diet, providing essential fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Ideal for

all age groups, this versatile powder can be used in baby food, porridge, smoothies, or even baking!

How to use

  • Mix 1-2 teaspoons of Raw Banana Powder with water or milk.
  • Cook for a few minutes until it thickens.
  • Sweeten with jaggery or honey if desired.
  • Enjoy it as porridge or add it to smoothies, pancakes, or baked goods.

Storage instruction:

Store in a cool, dry place away from moisture.

Keep the pack well-sealed after use.
