Red Banana Milk Mix

Introducing Theerthaa Red Banana Milk Mix – a wholesome and flavorful beverage mix crafted with naturally

ripened red bananas and traditional ingredients. Inspired by time-tested practices and mindful of today’s

need for clean, simple nutrition, this product brings together purity, taste, and convenience in every serving.


At Theerthaa, we focus on using ingredients that are known for their quality and origin. The red banana, a fruit

valued in many Indian households, is combined with natural jaggery to create a milk mix that suits people of

all age groups.


This product is ideal for those who prefer less-processed, naturally sweetened options and are looking to

include a traditional food element in their daily routine.

Why Choose Theerthaa Red Banana Milk Mix?

Naturally Ripened Red Bananas:

We use red bananas that are naturally ripened, ensuring better flavor and consistent quality.

These bananas are known for their rich color, smooth texture, and naturally sweet taste.

Minimal Ingredients, No Additives:

Our product contains only two ingredients: red banana and jaggery powder. There are no added

preservatives, artificial flavors, or synthetic colorings. This minimal processing allows the natural character of

the ingredients to shine through.

Product Composition:

Ingredients:

Red Banana

Jaggery Powder

There are no added sugars, thickeners, or fillers in this product. Jaggery is used as a natural sweetener, and the red banana is carefully dried and

powdered to preserve its flavor.

How to Use:

Making a glass of Red Banana Milk is quick and easy

  • Warm one glass of milk (do not boil).
  • Add two spoons of Red Banana Milk Mix.
  • Stir well until fully dissolved.
  • Serve warm.

No need to add sugar, as the jaggery provides a gentle sweetness. For best results, avoid adding the powder

while the milk is actively boiling.


This mix can also be stirred into warm plant-based milk alternatives, making it a flexible choice for different

dietary lifestyles.

Storage & Shelf Life:

To preserve freshness and flavor, follow these storage tips:

  • Keep in an airtight container after opening.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Use a dry spoon when scooping the mix.

Made for Every Generation

Theerthaa Red Banana Milk Mix is made with ingredients traditionally valued in Indian households. Red

By incorporating such ingredients into an easy-to-use format, this product bridges the gap between tradition

It is a great choice for families seeking to reintroduce age-old ingredients in a new, convenient form.
